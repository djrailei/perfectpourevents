Whiskey & Bourbon

Classic Standard

Old Fashioned

Bourbon · Angastura Bitters · Sugar · Orange Twist · Maraschino Cherry

Ingredients

2 oz Bourbon · 2 dashes Angostura Bitters · 1 Demerara Sugar Cube · Splash of Club Soda · Orange Twist · Maraschino Cherry

Method

1. Muddle the sugar and aromatic bitters directly in a heavy rocks glass until the sugar is dissolved (or combine the simple syrup and bitters if using syrup).
2. Add a single large ice cube.
3. Pour in the bourbon and stir gently until well chilled and properly diluted, about 20–30 seconds.
4. Express the oils from an orange twist over the drink, rub it around the rim, and place it in the glass.
5. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and serve.

Classic Standard

Bourbon Smash

Bourbon or Rye Whiskey · Simple Syrup · Lemon · Mint

Ingredients

2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey · 1 oz simple syrup · 1/4 of a lemon cut into two wedges · 3 or 4 fresh mint leaves

Method

1. Add the lemon wedges, simple syrup, and mint leaves to a mixing glass.
2. Muddle together.
3. Add bourbon and ice and stir.
4. Strain into a cocktail glass filled with ice and garnish with mint leaves.

House Signature

Bourbon Bang Bang

Bourbon · Aperol · Pinapple · Passion Fruit · Lime

Ingredients

2 oz Bourbon · 0.5 oz Aperol · 1 oz Pinapple · 1 oz Passion Fruit · 0.5 oz Lime Juice · Mint spring

Method

1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
2. Add the bourbon, Aperol, pineapple juice, passion fruit, and fresh lime juice.
3. Shake vigorously until well chilled.
4. Strain over fresh ice into a rocks glass.
5. Garnish with a fresh mint sprig and serve.
