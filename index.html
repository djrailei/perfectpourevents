The Cocktail Menu

Vodka Base

Classic Standard

Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 4 oz Artisanal Ginger Beer

Method

Combine ingredients in a traditional copper mug packed with crushed ice. Garnish with a fresh lime wheel.

House Signature

Lake Shore Lavender

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Premium Vodka
  • 0.75 oz Lavender-Infused Syrup
  • 0.75 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Club Soda float

Method

Vigorously shake with ice, double strain into a chilled coupe, and top with a gentle splash of carbonated club soda.

Tequila Base

Classic Standard

Classic Margarita

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Blanco Tequila
  • 1 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 0.75 oz Cointreau (Orange Liqueur)
  • 0.25 oz Agave Nectar

Method

Shake all ingredients thoroughly with ice. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice with a partial sea salt rim.

House Signature

The Ruby Paloma

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Reposado Tequila
  • 1.5 oz Fresh Ruby Red Grapefruit Juice
  • 0.5 oz Fresh Lime Juice
  • 0.5 oz Rosemary Simple Syrup
  • Top with Grapefruit Soda

Method

Shake tequila, juices, and rosemary syrup with ice. Strain into a highball glass over ice, top with grapefruit soda, and garnish with a torched rosemary sprig.

Whiskey & Bourbon

Classic Standard

Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Small Batch Bourbon
  • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 1 Demerara Sugar Cube
  • Splash of warm water

Method

Muddle sugar and aromatic bitters directly in a heavy rocks glass. Add a singular large cube, pour bourbon, and stir carefully.

House Signature

The Chicago Smoke

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Straight Rye Whiskey
  • 0.5 oz Sweet Italian Vermouth
  • 0.25 oz Ancho Reyes Chile Liqueur
  • Torched Rosemary sprig

Method

Stir liquid ingredients over ice. Strain into a custom glass infused with trapped, aromatic woodsmoke.
